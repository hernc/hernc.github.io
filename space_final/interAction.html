interACT

A project exploring interactions in a personal public space. Pushing one’s own boundaries & comfort. what questions which arise in ones mind could actually be answered by initiating interaction. names, careers, random thoughts, great stories & a sense of exploration & discovery….that’s what I thought

JOSALIE
1984
1:49

This particular girl told me that her Aunt was the one who named her. Her aunt combined her dad’s name (Jose) and her mom’s name (Lila) to form Josalie. She personally dislikes her name, because people pronounce it the wrong way all of the time. She wishes they would have went with something simple like Jolie or Lila.

thoughts:


 I though this was interesting, it’s rare I meet people who dislike their name as much as I do. Although my name is not terrible and can be easily pronounced, I just feel like there is always a Hispanic guy named carlos and 90 percent of the time it’s me!
