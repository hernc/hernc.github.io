DECISIONS

The angles 120º and 60º not used in the same order or placements throughout the typeface as other fonts. monospace parameters allow the stem to be the same weight when used as a diagonal or horizontal stem. The numbers used for the stems outline and inset play with the vision of the shadow letter and its dimension.
