JOSALIE 1984

This particular girl told me that her Aunt was the one who named her. Her aunt combined her dad's name (Jose) and her mom's name (Lila) to form Josalie. She personally dislikes her name, because people pronounce it the wrong way all of the time. They pronounce it Jocelyn or Ja-solie. She wishes they would have went with something simple like Jolie, or just something completely different. When I first asked her name she told me it was Josie, which is the name she prefers.

THOUGHTS:

I thought this was interesting, it's rare I meet people who dislike their name. I guess everyone could find a problem with his or her own name. Take me for example, although my name is not terrible and can be easily pronounced, I just feel like there is always a Hispanic guy named Carlos and 90 percent of the time it's me! However, I do like the fact that my name is never mispronounced and for short people have called me Carl or Los, which I do not mind.
